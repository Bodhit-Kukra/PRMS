PRMS (PATIENT RECORD MANAGEMENT SYSTEM)
INTRODUCTION
Hospitals around the world are receiving an overwhelming number of patients every day, making it difficult for staff of the hospitals to manage different patients’ test reports. The patient records management system makes it easy for staff of the hospitals to manage the patient details and patient test reports. The Patient Record Management System(PRMS) acts as an online tool that helps doctors to keep track of their patients and also check their patients’ status easily. It stores information of the patients like contact details, age, name etc. It also stores different health parameters of the patients. It will also help the new doctor in the event of absence of the old doctor of the patients by providing all the necessary details of the patients. It allows admins to store staff details and patient details for easy retrieval anytime, anywhere. It also allows staff at different labs to store the test results of patients online for easy access by the doctors.
FEATURES
  • Manage patient records
  • Manage staff records
  • Manage lab test results
  • Responsive UI
  • Responsive Dashboard
